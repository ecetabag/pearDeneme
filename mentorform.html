• The aim of Hisar Pear is to help younger students academically. We have all been there!

    This is how Hisar Pear Works:

    - The Hisar student fills out the form on the website to work with an older student for their class.

    - The student picks a time slot. - The mentor available during that time is assigned to the student. - They both receive an email with the Google Meet link, name of the student/mentor, and

    - They study for 50 minutes(th mentor can extend the time if needed) via Google Meet when the day comes.

    Important Points:

    - The students are between the grades 5 and 11. This is not a periodic tutoring. You will have a tutoring session only if a student needs help during your available times.

    - The mentors can always cancel their time slot if not available, and continue whenever they are free.

    - The mentors will receive a certificate of "Online Tutoring".
